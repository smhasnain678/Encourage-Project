About ENCOURAGE 2026

Empowering National Conference for Undergraduate Research

"Your Research Has a Voice — Let the World Hear It"

ENCOURAGE 2026 is Pakistan’s first large-scale platform dedicated to showcasing undergraduate research excellence. It brings together young scholars, innovators, and emerging leaders to present impactful ideas, collaborate with peers, and connect with academic and industry experts.

Organised by IRIS Education & Training Services, ENCOURAGE aims to promote high-quality academic research, inspire innovation, and empower students through national-level exposure and recognition.

Our Goals
  • Promote and strengthen undergraduate research culture in Pakistan.
  • Encourage inclusivity, collaboration, and diversity among students.
  • Support innovation, sustainability, and impactful real-world solutions.

Vision & Mission

Empowering National Conference for Undergraduate Research

"Believe in Your Ideas. They Can Change the Future"

Vision

To cultivate a vibrant culture of undergraduate research in Pakistan where every student is empowered, mentored, and encouraged to become a confident scholar, innovative thinker, and future leader contributing to national and global knowledge.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an accessible, supportive platform that enables undergraduate students to present their research, strengthen their academic profiles, and develop essential scholarly skills. Through conferences, workshops, mentoring, and collaborative learning, we aim to build students’ confidence, enhance their research capacity, and inspire their growth as emerging researchers, innovators, and professionals.
